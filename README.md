# fluid-frames

A startup engineering premium, customized phone frames from deconstructed legacy tech.

###Fluid Frames are not just wall decor; they are tangible portals to your personal history and digital nostalgia.
They capture the fleeting essence of the tech that shaped your life, freezing moments of innovation into permanent, striking art pieces. 
By deconstructing the devices that once held your memories, secrets, and daily connections, these frames honor the emotional weight of your digital journey.

🛠️ The Anatomy of Nostalgia
------------------------

- Preserved History: Old, beloved gadgets are carefully disassembled, laying bare the intricate craftsmanship hidden beneath the glass.
- Frozen Innovation: Every microchip, soldered wire, and motherboard is displayed like an artifact in a modern museum.
- Sentimental Value: The exact phone that carried your first love letters or late-night calls becomes a permanent visual anchor.
- Visual Storytelling: The layout tells a silent story of technological evolution and personal growth.

A Sensory Experience
--------------------

- The Tactile Contrast: Cold, metallic components sit beautifully framed behind smooth, protective glass.
- The Visual Rhythm: Dissected parts create a clean, geometric harmony that draws the eye and sparks conversation.
- The Quiet Reverence: Hanging a frame feels like giving a loyal digital companion a dignified, everlasting retirement.
